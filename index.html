Guide

How to use this tool

What does this tool do?

It helps you check your payout, bottom price, and buyer offer before selling a used item.

How to use

  1. Enter your selling price.
  2. Enter buyer offer if someone negotiates.
  3. Enter your minimum cash back.
  4. Add fees, shipping, and other costs.
  5. Check the result on the right.

How to read results

Bottom price means the price you should avoid going below. Net payout is what you keep after costs.

What are sold records?

If an item is sold, you can add it to records. Records are saved only in this browser.

Note

This is not an appraisal or market price tool. You enter all prices and decide whether to sell.

This tool is for personal calculation only. It is not an appraisal, receipt, invoice, or guaranteed selling price.
